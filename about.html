About the Magazine

After the Fall is a journal of fantasy that looks not to the sky, but to the ground beneath our feet — the moment after everything has broken.

We believe that the most powerful magic lives in the aftermath. In the silences between triumph and loss. In the spaces where wings no longer fly, yet the heart still beats. Our magazine exists to explore those fragile, fearless seconds when old stories end and new ones have not yet begun.

Each piece we publish invites you into worlds where wonder and wound are woven together. Where enchantment does not erase pain, but transforms it into something strangely beautiful. Where heroes are not defined by victory, but by the courage to take one more breath after the fall.

After the Fall is a home for dreamers who have known real darkness — and for the gentle, stubborn light that remains.

We do not promise easy answers or glittering happily-ever-afters. What we offer is truer: strange landscapes, aching echoes, and the quiet conviction that even in ruin, something new can grow.

Step into the space after the fall. You are welcome here.
